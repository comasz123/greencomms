    Activities

    Our focus is empowering journalists to fight misinformation and deliver fact-based information.

    We are volunteers, implementing various activities resources allow.

    Journalists Club

    Our flagship project is the TV Club journalists‚Äô community, founded in May 2019. Currently, the Network unites 194 media professionals interested in sustainable development, energy efficiency, quality of life, climate adaptation, conscious consumption, and participatory urban development. We are reaching millions via online and TV presence.

    Competitions

    Whenever resources allow, we organize internal competition for the best article from within the topics that are of interest for our members.     Events

    We organize high-level events connecting journalists with decision-makers. Many of them are under Hattam House roles to allow for free exchange of ideas. We are also building bridges between media professionals, civil society and business.

    Study trips

    Study trips to various locations in European Union, allow media professionals to get firsthand understanding of technologies and solutions which will be crucial in reconstruction and transformation of Ukraine to carbon-neutral.
